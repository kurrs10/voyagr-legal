Roam Wyld

The travel organizer for multi-country trips

Roam Wyld is an iOS app that helps travelers organize multi-destination trips in one place. Store trips and bookings, view per-passport visa and entry requirements, access country-specific emergency contacts, check live exchange rates, and import travel confirmations directly from Gmail.

Roam Wyld requests read-only Gmail access solely to find and import travel confirmation emails. Email data is processed to extract booking details and is not stored or used for any other purpose.

Trip & Booking Storage
Flights, hotels, activities in one place
Entry Requirements

Gmail Import

Emergency Contacts
